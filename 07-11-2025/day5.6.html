Forms allow users to interact with your website. They are used to collect data such as names, emails, feedback, or orders.

Important Form Elements:

  • form: — The container for input elements.
  • input: — Used for text boxes, radio buttons, and checkboxes.
  • textarea: — Allows users to type longer text or comments.
  • select: — Creates a drop-down list.
  • button: — Submits the form data.
