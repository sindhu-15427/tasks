Adding Audios and Videos in HTML

HTML allows you to add multimedia elements like audio and video directly into a webpage. This makes your content more engaging and interactive.

Important Tags

  • audio: — Used to embed sound files like music or narration.
  • video: — Used to display video clips.
  • source: — Defines the file source inside audio or video tags.
  • controls: — Attribute that adds play, pause, and volume options.

Example Uses:

  • Adding background music or podcasts.
  • Embedding educational videos or tutorials.
  • Sharing product demos or visual explanations.
