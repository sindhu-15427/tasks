Colors & Backgrounds

Explanation

  • Color changes text color.
  • Background-color fills background with solid color.
  • Linear-gradient creates a smooth color transition horizontally/vertically.
  • Radial-gradient creates circular color transitions.
  • Background-image sets an image as the background.
  • Background-size and position control how the image appears.
